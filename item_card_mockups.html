Goal: design a clearer "section structure" for item tooltips so players can parse Type · Damage · Implicit · Affixes · Modifier · Enchant · Flavor at a glance, especially at high rarity where lines pile up.

Each variant below renders the SAME three items at three power tiers (Common ⇒ Rare ⇒ God-tier 0.00001% Primal Masterwork Legendary) so you can compare how each layout scales from sparse to dense.

Hold Alt on the keyboard, or click the alt badge on a card, to expand affix detail (mirrors the in-game behavior). The global toggle below flips every card on the page.

All data: real items.json + affixes.json shapes — verified against item_tooltip.gd.
