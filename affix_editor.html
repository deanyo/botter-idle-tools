Affixes

Select an affix on the left, or click + New affix to create one.

Enchants are flavor-tag rolls that drop on items via enchant_chance. They drive combat procs (vampiric lifesteal, fire DoT, holy +vs undead, etc), the tooltip glow color, and the per-tag passive bonuses on worn gear. Combos appear when a drop rolls TWO enchants from the pool and the pair has a registered compound — the compound replaces both individuals, gets a unique combat effect, and renders with a blended color. Read-only view; effect handlers live in actor.gd + EnchantCombos.gd.
